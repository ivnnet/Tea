Rules

All UK citizens must drink tea at 10 AM, 3 PM, and 7 PM daily. Failure to comply results in a £50 fine for the first offense, a £200 fine and a mandatory tea education course for the second offense, and up to 3 months in jail for the third offense. Notifications must be enabled, and participation is legally required.
