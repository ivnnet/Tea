What is the Tea Time Alarm?

The Tea Time Alarm is a mandatory daily reminder for all UK citizens to drink tea at the scheduled times: **10 AM, 3 PM, and 7 PM**. Failure to participate can result in **fines or even jail time**.

Legal Requirements

It is a legal requirement to follow Tea Time rules. Failure to adhere to these regulations may result in legal consequences. This includes:

  • Fines for non-compliance.
  • Possible jail time for repeated violations.
  • Additional penalties imposed by authorities.

By accepting the Tea Time Pledge, you acknowledge and agree to comply with these legal requirements.

Fines and Penalties

  • First offense: **£50 fine**
  • Second offense: **£200 fine + mandatory tea education course**
  • Three offenses: **Possible jail time** (Up to **3 months**)

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I miss tea time?

If you miss tea time, you could face a fine or additional penalties. It’s important to stay on schedule!

Can I drink other beverages instead of tea?

No, the Tea Time law specifically mandates tea to be consumed during these hours.

How do I know when the next tea time is?

The countdown timer on this site will always show you the time remaining until the next tea time.
