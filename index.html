What is the Tea Time Alarm?

The Tea Time Alarm is a mandatory daily reminder for all UK citizens to drink tea at the scheduled times: 10 AM, 3 PM, and 7 PM. Failure to participate can result in fines or even jail time.

Fines and Penalties

  • First offense: £50 fine
  • Second offense: £200 fine + mandatory tea education course
  • Three offenses: Possible jail time (Up to 3 months)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why is Tea Time mandatory?

Tea time is part of the cultural heritage of the UK, and it is mandatory to ensure that everyone participates for health and community bonding.

What happens if I miss tea time?

If you miss tea time, you may face fines or be required to attend a tea education course.

Can I opt out of the Tea Time Alarm?

No, participation is mandatory for all citizens.
