Frequently Asked Questions

The Tea Time Alarm is a mandatory daily reminder for all UK citizens to drink tea at the scheduled times: 10 AM, 3 PM, and 7 PM. Failure to participate can result in fines or even jail time.

If you miss a tea time, penalties may apply. The first offense results in a £50 fine, the second offense in a £200 fine, and three offenses may lead to jail time.

Simply visit our website and accept the pledge to commit to drinking tea at the scheduled times.
