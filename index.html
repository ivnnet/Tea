What is the Tea Time Alarm?

The Tea Time Alarm is a mandatory daily reminder for all UK citizens to drink tea at the scheduled times: 10 AM, 3 PM, and 7 PM. Failure to participate can result in fines or even jail time.

Fines and Penalties

  • First offense: £50 fine
  • Second offense: £200 fine + mandatory tea education course
  • Three offenses: Possible jail time (Up to 3 months)
